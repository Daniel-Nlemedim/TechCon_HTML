Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for sharing innovation, collaboration, and future-facing tech ideas. Each year, thousands of developers, entrepreneurs, and tech leaders gather to exchange ideas and launch the next big thing.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower innovation, elevate diverse voices in technology, and connect builders from all around the world. We aim to create an inclusive space where ideas turn into action and tech becomes a force for good.
